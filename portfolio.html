Doodle Pop

A computerised version of Pictionary

As part of my A-Level Computer Science coursework, I designed a computerised version of Pictionary. During this project, I had to complete a write-up, detailing everything from start-to-finish, including market research, stakeholder analysis, system design, development, testing and evaluation. While this gave me practical experience, it more interestingly opened my eyes to the amount of detail which goes into any development.

I really enjoyed creating this product, along with, collecting feedback and working with my friends, who were my stakeholders, to test it's capabilities.

I warmly invite you to give it a try, in addition to providing me with any feedback. You can contact me at: d.j.lievesley@se25.qmul.ac.uk
